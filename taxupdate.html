Tax Updates

1. New Income Tax Regime – 2025

The Government has introduced revised income tax slabs offering reduced rates with fewer deductions. Taxpayers can choose between old and new regimes.

2. GST Annual Return – Due Date Reminder

All registered businesses are reminded to file their GST annual return (Form GSTR-9) by July 31st to avoid penalties.

3. Section 80C Limit Increased

The deduction under Section 80C is proposed to be increased from ₹1.5 lakh to ₹2.5 lakh in the upcoming financial year, encouraging more savings.

4. TDS e-Verification Rule

Taxpayers making TDS deductions above ₹50,000 must now complete mandatory e-verification within 7 days of payment.

5. ITR Portal Revamp

The Income Tax Department has upgraded the ITR e-filing portal with improved speed, real-time status updates, and simplified filing steps.
